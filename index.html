    I am currently studying Data Science at MMU. So far, I have completed units covering typical Data Science practises such as data loading and cleaning. I have also learnt about various classification and regression models and experimented with fitting them to large datasets. I have been able to build models that predict target features with reasonable accuracy. I am now learning about neural networks. I have used various Python libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Keras and TensorFlow.
